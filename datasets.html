Datasets

Throughout the years I've had to create different datasets for different things. A lot of them are pretty boring, but here's a few that are cool!:

U.S.D.A. Pomological Watercolors

Description: Watercolor collection documenting fruit and nut varieties developed by growers or introduced by USDA plant explorers around the turn of the 20th century.

National Geographic Magazine Covers

Description: Covers of all National Geographic Magazines, (1960-2018).

Bob Ross Paintings

Description: All Bob Ross paintings (and a few from his son) featured in the TV Show The Joy of Painting

Skate Video Music

Description: Dataset covering the music used in skateboarding videos from 1989 to 2018. Data scraped from skatevideosite.com
