Color Picker

If you're anything like me, you probably spend a lot of time selecting the right color for your charts.Choosing a color palette for a visualization can be surprisingly time consuming. Here's a tool to help ameliorate the problem: Given two colors, 600 color-values will be interpolated between the two. Clicking on a color will decrease its opacity. Hovering over a color will yield its hexidecimal representation.
